About Experience Specialities

Dr. Varun Palanati is a distinguished gastroenterologist and gastrointestinal surgeon based in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, offering a wide range of treatments for gastrointestinal disorders. His clinical expertise in colorectal surgery and surgical gastroenterology has made him one of the leading specialists in the region.

Dr. Varun's extensive education and training began with his MBBS from NRI Medical College, followed by an M.S. in General Surgery, and an M.Ch in Surgical Gastroenterology. His comprehensive knowledge allows him to offer both non-surgical and surgical solutions for gastrointestinal diseases, tailored to the unique needs of each patient.

Dr. Varun Palanati has an impressive career, highlighted by advanced surgical expertise and a deep commitment to patient care. His professional journey includes valuable experience at top-tier institutions such as Yashoda Hospitals, where he served as an Associate Consultant in the Department of Liver Transplant and Hepatobiliary Surgery.

Throughout his career, Dr. Varun has performed numerous complex gastrointestinal surgeries, ranging from liver resections to colorectal cancer treatments, making him one of the most sought-after specialists in the field.

At Shri Hospital, Dr. Varun Palanati specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of gastrointestinal conditions. His expertise extends across diseases of the liver, gallbladder, pancreas, and colorectal areas.

He is particularly skilled in minimally invasive and laparoscopic surgeries, ensuring faster recovery and minimal discomfort for patients. His holistic approach to patient care involves collaboration with multidisciplinary teams to provide comprehensive treatment plans for optimal results.
